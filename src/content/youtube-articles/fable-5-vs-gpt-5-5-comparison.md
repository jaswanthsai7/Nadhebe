# Claude Fable 5 vs GPT-5.5: The Battle for AI Model Dominance

Anthropic's June 2026 release of **Claude Fable 5** has set off a direct confrontation with OpenAI's **GPT-5.5** in the race for model supremacy. Optimized for long-horizon agentic reasoning, code synthesis, and deep logic operations, Fable 5 represents a major leap in cognitive automation.

## The Context and Pricing Landscape

To understand Claude Fable 5, it is essential to look at its underlying specs and target audience. Anthropic has structured Fable 5 as a high-capacity model designed for production workflows that demand comprehensive codebase ingestion:

* **1 Million Token Context Window**: Enables loading entire directories or massive multi-page documents without needing vector retrieval approximations.
* **128k Output Tokens**: Supports generating extensive scripts or long-form documentation blocks in a single execution step.
* **API Pricing Model**: Positioned as a premium reasoning tool at **$10.00 per million input tokens** and **$50.00 per million output tokens**.

By comparison, GPT-5.5 operates with faster processing rates and lower baseline compute requirements, making it the preferred model for quick chat actions, while Fable 5 is reserved for heavy multi-stage operations.

## Claude Fable 5 vs. Claude Mythos 5

A key point of confusion in the developer community concerns the relationship between Fable 5 and **Claude Mythos 5**:

* **Claude Fable 5**: The widely available production model. It contains Anthropic's full suite of safety classifiers, which prevent execution of requests related to cybersecurity exploits or biological hazards.
* **Claude Mythos 5**: Shares the exact same raw reasoning engine as Fable 5 but operates without the safety filter layers. Mythos 5 is highly restricted, accessible only to selected research partners through the **Project Glasswing** program.

### Regulatory and Access Timeline
Fable 5 experienced a brief suspension in mid-June 2026 due to U.S. government export controls regarding foreign access. Access was officially restored on **July 1, 2026**, following compliance upgrades.

## Performance Profiles

* **GPT-5.5**: Retains a slight advantage in raw generation speeds and basic logic tests (e.g. GSM8k), returning standard API responses in milliseconds.
* **Claude Fable 5**: Excels in multi-file dependency mapping, code reviews, and complex system-design architecture. Developers often combine the two, using GPT-5.5 for routine UI tasks and Fable 5 for structural backend analysis.
